Rules & Scoring

Everything you need to know about VPL Season 01

Batting Points
Runs
1 point per run
Runs Bonus
5 points for 25, 10 more points for reaching 50, 15 more points for reaching 75, and so on.
Strike Rate
Number of runs minus Number of balls faced. Doubled if positive, halved if negative.
Boundaries
One additional point per four. Three additional points per six.
Duck
Minus twenty for getting out for a duck.
Example Calculation
Player scores: 60 runs (45 balls), 4 fours, 2 sixes
Runs: 60 × 1 = 60 pts
Bonus (50): 5 + 10 = 15 pts
Strike Rate: (60 - 45) × 2 = 30 pts
Boundaries: (4 × 1) + (2 × 3) = 10 pts
Total: 60 + 15 + 30 + 10 = 115 points
Bowling Points
Wickets
30 points per wicket
Wicket Bonus
5 points for taking 2 wickets, 10 additional points for a third wicket, 15 additional points for a fourth wicket, and so on.
Economy Rate
The difference between the number of overs bowled, at 8.5 Runs per Over, and the number of runs conceded. Double if positive.
Dot Balls
1 point each
Maidens
15 points for a maiden over
Example Calculation
Bowler: 4 overs, 2 maidens, 24 runs, 3 wickets, 12 dot balls
Wickets: 3 × 30 = 90 pts
Bonus (3 wkts): 5 + 10 = 15 pts
Economy: (4 × 8.5 - 24) × 2 = (34 - 24) × 2 = 20 pts
Dot Balls: 12 × 1 = 12 pts
Maidens: 2 × 15 = 30 pts
Total: 90 + 15 + 20 + 12 + 30 = 167 points
Fielding Points
Catches
15 points
Run Outs
15 points
Stumpings
20 points
Squad Changes
  • In between regular season rounds, team owners are allowed to complete unlimited number of changes.
Captains
  • You are able to select a Captain who will earn you double points.
  • If your captain scores negative points, that will still be doubled.

Choose your captain wisely! A good captain can make the difference between winning and losing.

Emergencies
  • You have an extra player per playing position, who will act as an emergency if a player in your starting XI for that position doesn't play.
  • The emergency player's points will only count to your score after a player in that position has not played in their team's match for that round.
  • Players that are listed in the team when the game starts will count, even if they score 0 points or they never get the chance to take to the field due to rain, so long as a ball has been bowled in that match.
  • If a game is abandoned before the toss or before a ball has been bowled, those players are not considered to have played a game.
League Structure
  • Each round you will play head-to-head against another team or teams in your League, with teams ranked on a ladder based off those results.
Ladder
  • You get 4 points for a win, 2 for a draw, and no points for a loss.
  • Teams are then ordered by most points, then by percentage (points scored in matches as a percentage of points conceded).
